BUILDING INFORMATION MODELLING

BIM (or Digital integration implementation) creates and manages digital versions of buildings or infrastructure. BIM is at the forefront of AEC tech and can help you achieve a “golden thread of information”. Its collaborative approach to design, construction, and project management surpasses traditional 2D drawings, incorporating a 3D BIM model and a wealth of detail, from structural supports to sockets; BIM manages it all.
